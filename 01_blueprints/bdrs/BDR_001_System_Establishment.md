## 1. Status & Metadata
* **BDR ID:** BDR_001
* **Date/Timestamp:** 2026-05-29 19:15 UTC
* **Type:** Initial Foundation Strategy Choice
* **Impacted Scope Tracks:** Spawns and governs BREQ_001, BREQ_002, and BREQ_003

## 2. Context & Problem Statement
The human operator requires a high-fidelity multi-agent lifecycle ecosystem where human design intent cannot be diluted, missed, or corrupted during downstream handoffs between separate agent profiles.

## 3. The Business Decision (The "What")
We establish an absolute, unchangeable macro strategic baseline logic layer completely decoupled from functional product code. No feature requirements may exist within the ecosystem without an explicit ancestral link back to an authorized commercial or organizational business choice.

## 4. Strategic Rationale & Consequences (The "Why")
* **Context Preservation:** Tracks the high-level business motivation ("Why") behind all upcoming features, preventing automated downstream agents from dropping rules during handoffs.
* **Velocity Shielding:** Establishes a defensive governance boundary that forces immediate system intervention when informal engineering or product conversations drift into historical rule violations.
